Detailed Description

An H.266 NAL header (RFC 9328 §1.1.4 / VVC spec §7.3.1.2).

Bit layout, transmitted in network byte order: 

+---------------+---------------+
|0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7|
+-+-+-+-+-+-+-+-+-+-+-+-+-+-+-+-+
|F|Z| LayerID   |  Type   | TID |
+---------------+---------------+
